My Movie Reviews

Avengers: Infinity War

The Avengers of Earth and the Gardians of the Galaxy team up to stop Thanos from obtaining all six Infinty Stones, resulting which results in half of all life in the universe being wiped out.

My Review

The movie made me go through a roller-coaster of emotions. From hilarious dialogue and interactions from characters to a heartbreaking ending that leaves the future to be unknown.

Director Anthony Russo said:

"We like movies that are going to have a great sense of fun and thrill to them while at the same time remain very grounded in relatable human emotions and conflicts that the audiences can get behind and root for and empathise with."
